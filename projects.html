Our Projects

Over the years we have tried to put in place several projects to expand God's Kingdom in Kanana, a predominantly Islamic area

School

Three years ago we attempted to launch a project to educate children in the community, establish an affordable in-school feeding programme for them and slowly instill a culture of learning in the community but we had to drop the project because of lack of finances...

Rehabilitation Facility

Drug abuse is at an all-time high in Kanana and the sorrounding areas. We have attributed this to the pervasive poverty and lack of awareness in the area. In response to this situation, we are trying to put up a rehabilitaion center that will offer free or highly subsidized recovery services for drug abusers. This project is still in its infancy and your support is highly welcome.

Holiday Cottages and Conference Facilities

We are planning to put up holiday cottages and conference facilities for people from all corners of God's Kingdom. These facilities will help us earn enough income to support our mission.

Lend a hand

Kanana Fellowship Church Ministries (KFCM for short) is a Christian Church located in a small town called Kanana, in Kenya's South Coast Region.
