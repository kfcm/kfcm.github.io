About Our Ministry

The Calling

Senior Pastor Kepher Luhombo's Calling

I first heard God's voice in 2006 when I was working as a Clerical Officer and Salesman for a dried-flowers firm in Nakuru, a town located in Kenya's Rift Valley region. God was compelling me to move to Mombasa, Kenya's famous coastal city. I did not know the nature of this sudden compulsion to leave my comfortable life behind. Like Jonah in the Bible I dismissed this voice at first. My own family's discouragement to move also helped me ignore this "voice".

I ended up moving to Mombasa, but not to serve God. I wanted to open my own flowers business since I had the skills. Little did I know that God, all-knowing, all-powerful and everywhere, had other plans for me (Jeremiah 29:11).

In the wake of the 2007/2008 post-election crisis, my business premise and entire stock got burnt to ashes one night. I lost my family and I's entire livelihood. In essence I had gone back to zero. I did not know what to do or how I was going to feed, clothe or house my family.

But in this state of uncertainty, fear and confusion I heard God's voice again. This time it was louder and clearer. God wanted me to serve Him. He led me to Ukunda Baptist Church in Ukunda, a town in Kenya's South Coast region. I served as a Deacon here for about two years until they decided to plant a church in Kanana, a town located far from Ukunda. Kanana is a small center with a predominantly muslim population and people who still follow their traditional way of life. This, to me, was the beginning of a challenging experience because I had to face the fact that people would automatically be resistant to Christian doctrines.

Undeterred and obedient to my God's calling I took up this Ministry. I do not regret this decision. Kanana has been my home ever since I set foot here and I am glad I am in a position to win more souls for Christ.

Amen.

-Senior Pastor Kepher Luhombo

Pastor Kepher is married to Lucy Weddy Luhombo and they have been blessed with five beautiful children: four girls and one boy.

Our Mission

To proclaim, witness and make known the manifold wisdom of God. - Eph 3:10

Our Vision

To reach the unreached with the Good News of the Gospel of Jesus Christ, especially the muslim community in Kanana, the entire Coast region and other parts of the world, by fulfilling the Great Commission as outlined in Matthew 28:18-20.

"My desire and prayer to the Church is that you be rooted and grounded in sound doctrine and not be tossed back and forth by the waves and blown here and there by every wind of teaching." - Eph 4:14

"So shall my word be that goes forth from my mouth. It shall not return to me empty but it shall accomplish that which I please, and it shall prosper in the purpose to which I sent it." - Isaiah 55:11
