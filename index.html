Our Sunday Services

Our Sunday services are open to anyone who is willing and ready to open themselves up to God's Word and the Holy Spirit. We begin with opening prayers before we move on to praise and worship, followed by prayers, introductions of new faces, testimonies and offerings. We then embark on teaching the Word, praying for the sick and poor in the community and calling out to those who need Salvation.

Our Adults Ministry

We have a comprehensive set of church programmes tailored towards adults. We believe that our overall goal of spreading Christianity to all corners of the world starts by touching every adult's life in one way or the other. By teaching Christian values to a parent an entire household benefits. This is the basis of our efforts in the area.

Our Children Ministry

The Bible has several references on how we ought to be like "the little children" to receive God's favor. We strongly believe that children are an important part of any church's membership and therefore strive to induce enough fun and interactivity into their lessons.
