How You Can Help

Ways You Can Help Suppport Our Mission

We live in an area where the majority of people are either Muslims or followers of traditional religions. Our mission is to win as many souls as possible for God's Kingdom and it's been a challenging experience. Your contribution is not just a donation. It will go straight into providing us with the necessary resources to build a strong foundation for God's Kingdom here in Kanana and the sorrounding areas. You can contribute money, equipment or people to help support us. Thank you.

Financial Giving

To help us financially please consider making your contribution by cash, cheque, Western Union Money Transfer or Paypal.

Equipment

Have a bunch of clothes, electronic equipment or musical instruments you don't use anymore? Consider sending it to us.

People

We're considering putting in place an exchange programme to connect us to other ministries from every corner of God's Kingdom.
